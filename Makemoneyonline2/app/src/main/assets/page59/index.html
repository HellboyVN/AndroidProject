Way No 59 - Discover Products

If you are a fashionista, a designer or just someone who has a great sense of style, you can get paid to help people by finding products they’ll love.

There are people around the world who need experts to help them in discovering products for decorating their house or office.

They also need suggestions on wearing appropriate clothes and matching accessories.

If you think you can help such people by providing them right recommendations, then you can make a good amount of money doing this.

Suitable For -

Anyone who has a great sense of style.

Skills Required - 

1. Must have deep expertise in fashion.

2. Knowledge of a variety of products and authentic seller merchants.

3. Ability to understand client’s need and creatively recommend the products.     
 
4. Up to date knowledge of current trends and styles.

How To Get Started - 

According to my research the best option is to submit an online application to become an expert on Operator.com.

What is Operator.com?

Operator is a community which helps people discover and buy the best products for themselves. 

As an expert at Operator, your job will be to use your passion and sense of style to find and share the best products suitable for clients’ requirements.

You can submit your application on the following URL:

https://operator.interviewed.com/jobs/experts

Expected Earnings - 

The amount you’ll earn is entirely flexible, most of it will depend upon the size of the assignment and your experience.
 
You’ll receive hourly payment for the job also you’ll earn extra commissions for great recommendations.
