Way No 20 - Start Copywriting

What is Copywriting?

Copywriting is writing copy for the purpose of advertising or marketing. The copy is meant to persuade someone to buy a product or influence their beliefs.

Suitable For - 

People who have the passion for writing. 

Skills Required -

Ability to write a sales copy in order to maximize product sales. 

Time Required For Starting Copywriting -

You will have to learn the concepts and psychology behind successful copywriting which might take time according to your learning speed. 

How To Monetize - 

1. Good copywriters are paid a handsome amount of money for writing a single copy. In the beginning, you might not earn much, but once you will become an expert then no one can stop you earning hundred thousands just by writing few hundred copies.

2. You can start by creating a sales copy for the product you are promoting. See the results and try to improve as much as possible.

3. You can also earn money by freelancing your copywriting skills.
