Way No 17 - Start TeeSpring Campaigns

What is TeeSpring.com?

Teespring is the best way to design and sell custom apparel online. You design a product and sell it. The sales money pays for the production of your design and we ship it to all of your buyers.

Suitable For - 

Artists, designers, painters.

Skills Required - 

1. Ability to conceive and create designs.

2. Ability to convert designs into digital format using tools such as Photoshop.

3. Ability to promote your designs on social media

Time Required Starting A TeeSpring Campaign - 

If your design is ready you can create teespring campaign within minutes.

How To Monetize - 

1. Select T-shirt color and size. Upload the design. 

2. Start promoting your T-shirt on social media and/or using paid ads.

3. More T-shirts you sell, more money you earn.
