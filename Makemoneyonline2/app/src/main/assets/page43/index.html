Way No 43 - Use ReceiptHog App

What is ReceiptHog?

ReceiptHog is a FREE mobile app that pays you to scan purchase receipts.

It’s a very cool way to make some money on the fly because just snapping your purchase receipts takes no time and work at all.

Taking pictures of your receipts, regardless of the length or source, earns you coins within the app which you can redeem for real cash.

Here’s how ReceiptHog works:     

  1. Download the app from iTunes or Google Play.
  2. Take pictures of your receipts from any store, mall, restaurant or cafe.
  3. Earn ReceiptHog rewards like coins, spins at the virtual slot machine, and sweepstakes prizes.
  4. Redeem coins with PayPal or Amazon gift cards. 

Now you might think, why would anyone pay you for such a service? 

Well, brands and retailers want to know what and how consumers purchase goods in order to refine their marketing strategy. 

Thus, as a member of ReceiptHog, you are indirectly participating in market research process which could influence future products and strategies.

Another great feature of ReceiptHog app is its virtual slot machine.

Here you can win extra coins, an instant $100, or even have your last shopping trip paid for.

You can also take extra surveys to earn additional coins.
